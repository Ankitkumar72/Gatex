# Policy: Trash & Recycling Disposal
**Document ID:** POL-TRASH-03

## 1. Locations
* **Trash Chute:** Bagged household trash only. Located near the elevator.
* **Recycling:** Blue bins in the basement garage. Flatten all cardboard.
* **Dumpster:** Large items (furniture) must go to the exterior dumpster.

## 2. Prohibited Items
* Do NOT put pizza boxes (grease) in recycling.
* Do NOT put electronics or batteries in regular trash.
* Do NOT leave bags on the floor of the trash chute room ($25 fine).

## 3. Pickup Schedule
* Valet Trash (if applicable): Place bin outside door by 8 PM Sunday-Thursday.
