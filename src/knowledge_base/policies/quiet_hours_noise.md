# Policy: Quiet Hours & Noise Control
**Document ID:** POL-NOISE-12

## 1. Standard Quiet Hours
* **Weekdays:** 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM.
* **Weekends:** 11:00 PM to 8:00 AM.

## 2. Acceptable Noise Levels
During quiet hours, noise should not be audible outside the unit door or through shared walls/floors.
* TV/Music volume must be lowered.
* No laundry or vacuuming.

## 3. Enforcement
* **1st Violation:** Written Warning.
* **2nd Violation:** $50 Fine.
* **3rd Violation:** Lease violation notice / Eviction grounds.
