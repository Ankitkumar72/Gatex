# Policy: Pets and Animals
**Document ID:** POL-PET-08

## 1. Approved Animals
* Dogs and Cats are allowed with prior written approval.
* Maximum: 2 pets per unit.
* Weight Limit: 50 lbs (ground floor units exempt).

## 2. Fees
* **Pet Deposit:** $300 (Refundable).
* **Pet Rent:** $35/month per pet.

## 3. Restrictions
* Aggressive breeds (Rotweilers, Pit Bulls, Dobermans) are prohibited by insurance policy.
* Exotic animals (snakes, ferrets, spiders) are strictly prohibited.
* **Waste:** Tenants must clean up immediately. Failure to do so results in a $50 fine per incident.
