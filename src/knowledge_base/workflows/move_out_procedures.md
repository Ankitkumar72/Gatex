# Workflow: Tenant Move-Out & Turnover
**Document ID:** WORK-MOVEOUT-02

## 1. Tenant Responsibilities
Before handing over keys:
* Unit must be "Broom Clean" (swept, trash removed).
* Fridge and cabinets emptied.
* All keys/fobs placed on kitchen counter.

## 2. Damage Assessment (Security Deposit)
* **Normal Wear & Tear:** Small nail holes, minor scuffs (No charge).
* **Chargeable Damage:** Large holes, carpet stains, broken blinds, deep scratches in hardwood.

## 3. Timeline
* Inspection must occur within 48 hours of vacancy.
* Deposit refund/itemized deduction list sent within 21 days.
