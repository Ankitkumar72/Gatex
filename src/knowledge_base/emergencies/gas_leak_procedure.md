# Emergency Protocol: Gas Leak Response
**Document ID:** EMER-GAS-002
**Severity:** Critical

## 1. Detection Signs
* **Smell:** "Rotten egg" / Sulfur odor.
* **Sound:** Hissing noise near pipes or appliances.
* **Physical:** Dead houseplants, dizziness, or nausea.

## 2. Strict Prohibitions
If you suspect a leak:
* **DO NOT** turn light switches on or off (spark risk).
* **DO NOT** use a landline phone or cell phone inside.
* **DO NOT** light matches or lighters.

## 3. Action Plan
1.  Evacuate all people and pets immediately.
2.  Call the Gas Utility Provider from outside the building.
3.  Call PropFlow Emergency Line.
