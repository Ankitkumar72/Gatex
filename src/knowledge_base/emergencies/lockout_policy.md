# Policy: Tenant Lockouts & Key Replacement
**Document ID:** EMER-KEY-04

## 1. Business Hours (9 AM - 5 PM)
* Come to the leasing office with a photo ID.
* A temporary key will be loaned for 1 hour.
* **Cost:** Free.

## 2. After Hours / Weekends
* Call the After-Hours Emergency Line.
* A maintenance technician will be dispatched to unlock the door.
* **Cost:** $75 "Lockout Fee" (charged to next month's rent).
* *Note:* Technician must verify ID before unlocking.

## 3. Lost Keys
* Key Replacement: $25 per key.
* Fob Replacement: $50 per fob.
* Lock Re-keying (if security compromised): $150.
